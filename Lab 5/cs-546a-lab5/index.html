Biography

As a child, I always wanted to be like Wolverine. I don’t mean the claws part (well that would be cool too!) but his invincibility. It always amazed me as to how after every injury, he would heal himself and get back to life like nothing happened. As a kid, I would fall, and get a lot of wounds and bruises. Then I wasn’t allowed to play until I have healed completely. And that’s why I wanted to be like Wolverine. This spirit of being invincible has stayed with me till date and it motivates me to never give up.
